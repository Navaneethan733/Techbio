      Our Journey

      Key Milestones in Our Story

      From a small research lab to a global biotech leader — here's how it happened.

      2009
      Company Founded

      STACKLY was established with a team of 12 scientists and a shared dream: to bring genomic medicine to the mainstream. Our first lab opened in Bangalore, India.

      2012
      First Clinical Partnership

      Signed our first major partnership with a leading oncology hospital to conduct Phase II genomic trials across 500 patients — producing landmark data on targeted therapy response rates.

      2015
      AI Platform Launch

      Launched our proprietary AI-powered genomics analysis platform, reducing sequence analysis time from 72 hours to under 4 hours — a 18x improvement in throughput.

      2018
      Global Expansion

      Opened research centres in the USA, Germany, and Singapore. Achieved ISO 17025 certification and began serving pharmaceutical clients across 25+ countries.

      2021
      CRISPR Breakthrough

      Published landmark research on CRISPR-based gene correction for inherited metabolic disorders — cited by Nature Medicine as one of the top 10 biomedical breakthroughs of the year.

      2024
      250+ Projects Milestone

      Surpassed 250 active research projects globally, with a 95% clinical success rate. Named among the Top 20 Biotech Companies to Watch by BioTech World magazine.
